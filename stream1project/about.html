History of Laugh-A-Lot Team

  • 2014-2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Ammy Singh have a big interest of making a Punjabi music videos. So, mid of 2014 he discussed this idea with his old friend Arpit Khurana. After that they both decided to make a Punjabi music video. After all that, they both made the plan and then they filmed the Punjabi music video. In this music video Ammy Singh appeared as a lead actor.





  • Feb 2015

    Laugh-A-Lot Team is Born

    After the success of first video. We decided to make more videos. But this time we needed one more person. So we found one more talented person Raghav Khurana. Raghav advised us, we should try comedy videos on basis of Irish Indians. This can bring us huge audience. So, we made first ever comedy clip, its call SPEAK IN ENGLISH. We got very good response from audience. So, from here Laugh-A-Lot story begins...





  • March 2015 - Dec 2015

    BECOME PASSIONATE

    In between March 2015 to Dec 2015 we made lots of comedy videos. All of them were well accepted by audeience.





  • Jan 2016 till Now

    Back to Punjabi Music Videos

    In 2016, we wanted to make again some punjabi music videos. As all we know Ammy Singh is Singer aswell and he had couple of songs recorded. So, we decided to do our own project in the begin of the year.

    Than we filmed one of his beat song called, Bhangre da Shaukeen Gabru. It is in Punjabi language. It is a Bhangra dance beat. You can check his song in video section. After that we shooted another punjabi song called, Des sung by Ranjit Bawa. It is just a Fan made video.
